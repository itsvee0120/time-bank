## Get started

**Houreum** is a mobile-first community skill-sharing application built with React Native and Expo. It operates on the principle of a time bank, where the currency is time itself. Users can offer their skills and time to help others in the community and, in return, earn time credits that they can spend on services they need.

## üåü Core Features

```bash
npm install
```

- **Time-Based Economy**: Earn and spend time credits. One hour of your time equals one hour of someone else's.
- **Task Marketplace**: Browse, post, and accept tasks ranging from tutoring and graphic design to gardening and home repairs.
- **User Profiles**: Showcase your skills, availability (online, in-person, or both), and location to connect with your local community.
- **Task Management**: Keep track of your posted tasks ("My Requests") and the tasks you've committed to ("Current Requests").
- **Secure Time Reporting**: A simple and fair system for workers to report hours and for task owners to approve them, ensuring balances are updated correctly.
- **Push Notifications**: Stay updated with real-time alerts for task assignments, completions, and other important events.
- **Community Safety**: Features to report suspicious activity and bugs, helping to maintain a safe and functional platform.
- **Open Source**: The entire codebase is open for a transparent, community-driven development process.
